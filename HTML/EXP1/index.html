The CityPool is designed to provide accurate and up-to-date information about public transportation services. It helps users plan their journeys efficiently by offering route details, schedules, stop information, and service updates.

The portal aims to improve commuting convenience, reduce travel uncertainty, and encourage the use of public transport for sustainable urban development.
